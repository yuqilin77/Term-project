About me

Hello! Welcome to my channel! My name is Yuqi, but you can also call me Yuki. I am delighted to have this opportunity to introduce myself and am honored to be one of the students in course CS 601.

I come from China, but I currently reside in San Francisco Bay Area. I have been in U.S. for 8 years. Prior to moving to San Francisco, I lived in New Orleans and Houston. Professionally, I have a background in finance and accounting. I graduated from Tulane University with Master of Accounting degree. I got my CPA licence two years after I graduated from Tulane. I have been working in the financial industry for around seven years. I worked at Ernst & Young as an audit senior for over 2 years, and currently, I am working in a hedge fund investment company as a fund accountant in San Francisco.

In addition to my professional endeavors, I also enjoy coding and learning about software development. Technology is changing our lives, and I am motivated to acquire more IT and software skills to enhance my work efficiency and bring technology into financial field. While I appreciate back-end development, I have a preference for front-end work because it offers more interactivity. My goal is to become an accountant who excels in coding and programming skills.
